Pudge

Pudge is the sound of sludge-drenched rage rising from the depths — a fusion of groove-laden riffs, doom-soaked atmosphere, and nu metal rawness, forged in the heart of heaviness. Their music grinds forward like a rusted machine fueled by pain, cynicism, and the ghosts of a broken world.

With chugging guitars, growling basslines, and guttural roars that echo through concrete and smoke, Pudge delivers a sound that is both primal and hypnotic. Their themes dive into urban decay, mental collapse, vengeance, and the weight of existence, wrapped in distortion and dread. Brutal yet groovy, slow but unforgiving, Pudge doesn’t just play music — they drag you into a pit of sound where every riff feels like a swing of a sledgehammer, and every silence is heavier than the last.
