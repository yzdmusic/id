Buriajoath

Buriajoath is an extreme deathcore band from Indonesia that delivers raw, unfiltered aggression through heavy riffs, chaotic blast beats, and crushing breakdowns. Their sound is violent and relentless, with a vocal approach that fuses shrieking highs and guttural lows. Lyrically, they dive deep into themes of inner torment, revenge, death, and spiritual decay — all expressed in harsh, street-style language that reflects real pain and lived darkness.

Known for their disturbing visuals and uncompromising aesthetic, Buriajoath embraces gore, decay, and horror as essential parts of their identity. Their music is not just a sonic assault, but a form of resistance — a weapon for the broken, the angry, and the outcast. In the world of Buriajoath, there is no escape from the nightmare — only the chance to scream louder than the silence.
