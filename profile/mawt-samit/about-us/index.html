Mawt Samit

Mawt Samit is a relentless force of apocalyptic sound — a fusion of brutal death metal ferocity and sweeping symphonic darkness. Born from the chaos of mortality and myth, their name — meaning "Silent Death" — speaks to the cold finality at the heart of their music.

With orchestral arrangements that echo like funeral processions, and guttural growls that tear through the void, Mawt Samit unleashes a sonic experience both majestic and merciless. Their lyrics descend into realms of existential dread, ancient doom, and spiritual extinction, wrapped in melodies that haunt and devastate. Combining cinematic scale with bone-crushing intensity, Mawt Samit is not merely a band — it is a ritual of sonic destruction, crafted for those who embrace the darkness with reverence and rage.
