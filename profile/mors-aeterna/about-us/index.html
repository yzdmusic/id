Mawt Samit

Mors Aeterna is a dual-vocalist deathcore band that drags listeners into the heart of darkness — where beauty meets brutality and life dances with death. Born from the ashes of grief and defiance, the band's name, meaning "Eternal Death", defines their vision: unending chaos, emotional abyss, and sonic warfare.

Fronted by two commanding voices — a deep, guttural male growler and a fierce, venomous female screamer — Mors Aeterna forges a dynamic contrast of rage and agony, control and madness. Their songs explore death, trauma, divine punishment, inner destruction, and metaphysical collapse — all wrapped in punishing breakdowns, dissonant riffs, and atmospheric interludes that whisper from the void. Their sound is cold, raw, yet sophisticated — merging the violence of deathcore with ambient textures and theatrical transitions. Each track is a lamentation and a rebellion. Their live shows are ritualistic experiences, echoing with despair and fury as both vocalists command the stage like apocalyptic prophets. Mors Aeterna is more than a band — it's an invocation. A requiem for humanity. A scream from the afterlife.
