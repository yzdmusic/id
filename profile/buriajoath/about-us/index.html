Buriajoath

Buriajoath stands as a brutal force within the deathcore underground, channeling unfiltered aggression through slashing riffage, chaotic blast beats, and crushing breakdowns. Their vocal assault shifts between piercing shrieks and guttural lows, capturing a raw intensity that pulls no punches. In tracks like “Twisted Vengeance” and “Hellbound Bitches,” they forge a visceral soundscape that is as punishing as it is unrelenting — an auditory maelstrom for fans of uncompromising extremity.

Lyrically, Buriajoath dives deep into bleak realms of inner torment, revenge, death, and spiritual decay, articulating these themes with grim, street-level immediacy. Songs such as “Pig Blood Promises,” “Snap And Slay,” and “Burn The Whores Forever” showcase their dark cinematic flair, painting macabre tales of suffering and rage through unapologetically harsh language. This is deathcore stripped to its bone — relentless, cathartic, and fiercely authentic.
