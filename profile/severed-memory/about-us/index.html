Severed Memory

Severed Memory is a relentless force in the hardcore underground, known for crashing riffs and emotionally raw power. Their sound is defined by soaring yet aggressive melodies, thunderous drumming, and vocals that channel both rage and vulnerability.

With popular tracks like Hollowed Symphony, Shattered Out, and Nothing Is Beautiful, the band tackles themes of inner turmoil, loss, and defiance. Though fictional, Severed Memory’s music resonates as if born from real pain — a sonic catharsis that invites listeners to confront their darkest memories and emerge stronger.
