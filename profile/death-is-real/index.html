About

Verified artist

Death Is Real emerges as a visceral embodiment of raw, unbridled aggression within the deathcore realm. Their sonic signature—an unrelenting maelstrom of guttural growls, piercing pig squeals, and pulverizing breakdowns—delivers an intense auditory assault designed to unshackle listeners from complacency. This band, deeply rooted in underground aesthetics, thrives on a garage-like ethos, where every note feels knotted with tension, every riff drenched in rage, and every performance is a sonic invocation of chaos.
