Manticore

Manticore is a hardcore band known for its raw intensity, aggressive sound, and emotionally charged atmosphere. Their music blends fast, crushing riffs with relentless drum patterns and powerful vocals, creating a sonic assault that reflects anger, struggle, and inner conflict. Rooted in the hardcore scene, Manticore delivers a sound that is both brutal and expressive, capturing the essence of modern hardcore with a dark and uncompromising edge.

Lyrically, Manticore explores themes such as isolation, emotional pressure, resilience, and personal battles, making their songs resonate deeply with listeners who connect to honest and confrontational narratives. Through releases like Alone In The Silence and tracks such as Burn It All To Death and Nothing Left But Fire, the band has built a strong presence among hardcore fans, standing out for their intensity, sincerity, and unwavering dedication to heavy music.
