Dorokdox Symphony

Dorokdox Symphony is a fictional band that masterfully blends the intensity of Symphonic Metal, Heavy Metal, Gothic Metal, and Screamo. Their music is characterized by powerful male vocals, intricate orchestral arrangements, and dark, emotive themes.

Their discography includes albums like Revenge Projection (2025), Democrazy (2025), and Afterlife (2020), featuring tracks such as "Kill Them," "End Of Days," and "Goatman." These works showcase their ability to merge aggressive guitar riffs with symphonic elements, creating a sound that's both haunting and exhilarating.
