Chains And Checkers

Chains And Checkers is the sound of resistance — a fierce blend of heavy metal brutality, hardcore urgency, punk rebellion, and the infectious bounce of ska. With razor-sharp riffs, pounding drums, and offbeat grooves, they tear through the noise with a sound that's both politically charged and wildly explosive.

Fueled by frustration and forged in the underground, the band delivers raw anthems about freedom, identity, injustice, and survival, shouting truths over chaos and rhythm. Their music shifts between headbanging rage and skanking release — aggressive but never without purpose. Chains And Checkers isn’t just a name — it’s a symbol of control and contrast, of order and defiance. For those who live loud and think louder, this band is your anthem and your alarm clock.
