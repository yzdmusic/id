After Breakfast

After Breakfast is pure chaos served loud — a genre-bending explosion of thrash metal aggression, alternative unpredictability, and the wild, offbeat energy of ska. Their sound is raw, fast, and defiant, like a punk-fueled riot crashing into a metal show at full speed.

Shredding riffs collide with upstroke guitars, driving basslines bounce beneath manic vocals, and every track feels like a punchline delivered with a baseball bat. With lyrics full of irony, rebellion, and biting humor, After Breakfast is a voice for the disillusioned and the loud. They don’t follow the rules — they mock them, break them, and skank over the pieces. For fans of mosh pits, unpredictability, and musical madness, After Breakfast is more than a band — it’s a morning ritual of mayhem.
